# Geotech Exploration Equipment Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation of Geotech Instrument, a professional manufacturer of geophysical exploration equipment.

## About Us
We design and manufacture high-precision instruments for mineral exploration, groundwater detection, engineering geology and archaeological survey.

## Core Products
- **ERT Resistivity Meter**: Distributed high-density electrical imaging system
- **Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR)**: Multi-frequency GPR for pipeline detection and concrete scanning
- **Proton Magnetometer**: High-precision magnetic survey instrument for ore exploration
- **Seismic Nodes**: Wireless seismic acquisition system
